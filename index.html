The ramblings of Mr. Nobody

Welcome to the mind of Mr. Nobody. This is the first post of many. For updates you can follow the Twitter account of Mr. Nobody. If you want to send any suggestion or you are having some issue with the website send him an email. Nobody will answer.

Profile of Mr. Nobody

Mr. Nobody knows many things, none of them in depth. He has a natural tendency for abstract thinking, art and irony. Irony helps to deal with the intrinsic paradox of the world, and to avoid taking himself too seriously.

At an early stage his life oscillates between joy and depression. Later on he becomes a highly detached individual (in a materialistic sense) but yet he desperately seeks for some kind of truth or purpose. This detachment doesn't necessarily imply egoism: Mr. Nobody likes helping others just for the sake of helping.

I've said he but you, lady, human being and one day maybe robots, if you're reading this, you might belong to the specie.

Why

Mr. Nobody believes in scientific reasoning -even though he usually fights against it. By overthinking Mr. Nobody came to the conclusion that life has no purpose. He has discovered -or he supposes he has discovered, or something, that there is no why: the world just happens, life just happens. I am pushed towards the writing of this letters, and you, reader, to read them.

Mr. Nobody is an absurdist but as a side effect he seeks for meaning on every chink of the world.

This website

I am another puppet, an example case. I've studied a little bit of music, literature, philosophy, languages, science, programming. This website is an attempt to show what's going on inside this kind of mind.

Mr. Nobody
