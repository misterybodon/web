The Person

My name is Santiago Miranda. But it doesn't differs from Mr. Nobody; and the last one fits better in me.

These days I've been travelling. This is a good opportunity for reborns. When we arrive to a new place nobody knows us, then we can be whatever we want. Mr. Nobody is addicted to it. (Apologizes if I mix first and third person, sometimes I find it easier to take distance of the first person when writing or even when speaking.) I don't practize this reborns as intensely as I dream of, but it's getting closer with time.

But, but what's the point of it pal?

It is to understand what life is. For me, life is a moving marvel that must be seen from different angles to get a deeper understanding of it. [And we never get it all.] Every reborn opens up a new world of possibility, another door is open in the endless corridor.

I don't think there is a reason for this behaviour though, I just do it, and I Like It. I am an experiment, I feel the adreline of the unknown, fear and joy, life showing up to me on its own law and power.

Why not to use your real name then?

Mr Nobody is not just me. It's showing up a very common mindset and way of being from this times. With some variation, of course. Mr Nobody has troubles to understand Why life is as it is?, Why did it all start, Why there is so much suffering.

This is a bit off here. It's here though. There is a key test for Mr Nobody's detection. Do you take yourself seriously?
